# Prompt Seedance 2.0 — Vídeo Hero em Loop Perfeito

## Prompt principal

Create a premium cinematic 720p website hero background video for a digital marketing brand called R2R Marketing Digital. Use the attached reference visual identity: dark navy futuristic background, electric blue and purple neon circuit lines, premium technology atmosphere, performance marketing dashboards, artificial intelligence energy, and subtle glowing particles.

The video must be a perfect seamless loop: the first frame and the last frame must match exactly in camera position, lighting, object placement, particle distribution, and background composition. Duration 6 to 8 seconds. No audio. 16:9 aspect ratio, 720p.

Visual concept: a dark premium technology environment with blue and purple digital circuits slowly pulsing. Floating translucent marketing dashboard panels and data lines appear subtly and then return to their original state by the final frame. Keep motion elegant and minimal, suitable for a website hero background behind text. Use realistic lighting, no cartoon style, no fake AI artifacts, no unreadable text, no distorted logos, no warped phone screens, no duplicated phones, no extra hands, no random objects.

Camera: locked-off camera, no fast movements, no zoom jumps. Very slow parallax only, smooth motion, high-end commercial look. Lighting: deep navy shadows, cyan rim lights, purple glow, soft bloom, high contrast but not overexposed. Materials: glassmorphism dashboards, subtle reflections, realistic screen glow. The composition must leave a clean central area for website headline text.

Technical requirements:
- 720p
- 16:9
- 24 fps
- 6–8 seconds
- seamless loop
- no sound
- no subtitles
- no text overlays
- no watermarks
- no distorted UI
- no abrupt cuts
- no camera shake
- first frame identical to last frame

## Negative prompt

No AI-looking artifacts, no extra phones, no duplicated objects, no warped screens, no unreadable text, no random letters, no faces changing, no logo distortion, no glitch transitions, no heavy camera movement, no flickering, no low-resolution textures, no cartoon/anime look, no hands, no people unless already present in the reference and kept stable.

## Versão com foco em montagem/desconstrução de objeto

Use this version if the concept is an object being assembled/disassembled:

Create a cinematic 720p seamless loop hero background video. The reference object should begin fully assembled, then its parts separate in slow motion as if floating in a premium futuristic space, revealing internal components with clean mechanical precision. The parts then smoothly reassemble into the exact same fully assembled object by the final frame. The first and last frame must be identical for a perfect autoplay loop.

Style: premium product commercial, dark navy background, electric blue and purple rim lighting, realistic metal and glass materials, soft reflections, high-end studio lighting, subtle particles, no audio. Camera locked-off with very slight parallax only. The motion must be smooth, elegant and believable, with no explosions, no chaos and no unrealistic deformation.

Technical requirements: 720p, 16:9, 24 fps, 6–8 seconds, no sound, no text, no subtitles, no watermark, perfect loop, same first and last frame.
